About My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia (Boku no Hero Academia) is an anime series set in a world where nearly every human has a superpower known as a "Quirk." The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a young boy born without a Quirk, who dreams of becoming the world's greatest hero.

As he enters U.A. High School, the prestigious institution for aspiring heroes, Midoriya’s life is changed when he inherits the powerful quirk of the world’s greatest hero, All Might. Alongside his classmates, he faces dangerous villains and works toward his ultimate goal of becoming a symbol of peace.

Main Characters

Izuku Midoriya

The main protagonist, born without a quirk, but inherits All Might's powers.

Katsuki Bakugo

Izuku's childhood friend and rival, with a quirk that allows him to create explosions.

All Might

The greatest hero, who passes on his quirk to Izuku Midoriya.
