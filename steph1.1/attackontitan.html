About Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction. Enormous humanoid creatures known as Titans devour humans, and the remnants of mankind live within walled cities to protect themselves.

The story follows Eren Yeager, a young man whose life changes forever when his hometown is destroyed by the Titans. Vowing revenge, Eren, along with his friends Mikasa and Armin, joins the military to fight back against the Titans and uncover the mysteries of their existence.

Main Characters

Eren Yeager

The main protagonist, driven by a desire to eliminate all Titans and uncover the truth behind their existence.

Mikasa Ackerman

Eren's adoptive sister and one of the most skilled soldiers in the military, fiercely protective of Eren.

Levi Ackerman

Known as humanity’s strongest soldier, Levi is a key figure in the military's fight against the Titans.
